The Strategic Blueprint

Catalyst

I am building Catalyst. Catalyst isn’t just another dashboard. This platform serves as a strategic blueprint for using artificial intelligence to master the e-commerce supply chain, moving from initial discovery to final profit calculations.

The process begins with digital trend validation, where AI tools scan social platforms and search data to identify products experiencing rapid growth and high consumer intent. By conducting sentiment analysis and market gap checks, the system uncovers specific product flaws and unmet needs, allowing an entrepreneur to design a superior version of a trending item. Finally, Catalyst automates the sourcing and logistics phase, connecting with verified manufacturers and calculating margins to ensure a viable business opportunity.

Core Pillars

Predictive Product Discovery

Move beyond historical data. Predict emerging market gaps before they become saturated.

Proprietary AI that writes and live-tests listings that rank higher and convert faster.

Find authentic sourcing and calculate margins to ensure a viable business opportunity.
